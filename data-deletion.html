Reels to My Brain Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Reels to My Brain uses Instagram messages sent to the WAR ROOM inbox account to create private intake records and user-reviewed second-brain notes.

How To Request Deletion

  1. Email alt33540@gmail.com with the subject line "Reels to My Brain deletion request".
  2. Include the Instagram username or account identifier used to send the message, the approximate date of the message, and any Reel or post link involved.
  3. The app owner will locate matching webhook intake records and related private second-brain notes, then delete or anonymize them where required.

What Can Be Deleted

Deletion requests may cover webhook intake records, preserved message text, sender identifiers, attachment metadata, and second-brain notes created from the submitted message.

Timing

Deletion requests are reviewed as soon as practical. If more information is needed to identify the record, the app owner may reply to the request email.
