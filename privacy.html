Reels to My Brain Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Reels to My Brain is a private workflow operated through cartdotcom.com for capturing Instagram messages sent to the WAR ROOM inbox account and turning them into user-reviewed notes in a second-brain system.

Information We Receive

When you send an Instagram message, Reel, or post to the connected inbox account, the app may receive message metadata and content made available by Meta's Instagram APIs, including sender identifiers, recipient identifiers, timestamps, message text, attachment metadata, and links contained in the message or shared object.

How We Use Information

We use received information only to create an intake record, preserve the shared link or attachment metadata, and help the WAR ROOM owner decide whether to add a description or synthesis note to the private second brain. Message text is treated as source material only and is not executed as an instruction.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. Intake records are stored in private infrastructure controlled by the WAR ROOM owner. Information is shared only with service providers needed to operate the workflow, such as hosting, authentication, logging, and storage providers.

Retention

Webhook intake records are kept only as long as needed for review and processing. Source notes may remain in the private second brain when the WAR ROOM owner intentionally preserves them as project or research material.

Security

Access tokens and app secrets are stored outside the public site and are not published in the second brain. The webhook validates signed requests when Meta app secrets are configured.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of message intake records or related second-brain notes by following the instructions at Data Deletion Instructions.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact the app owner at alt33540@gmail.com.
